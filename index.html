Basic Mechanics

  • There are three players: one human player and two AI competitors.
  • Each player always has three cards in their hand.
  • Each round, every player plays one card (either an Action card or an Event card).
  • After playing a card, each player draws a new card to replace it.
  • Action cards have no direct effect other than being played.
  • Event cards have different effects depending on which Action cards were played.
  • For example, an event card might:
  • Award progress points to players who have played a particular action card
  • Cause players to lose progress points unless they have played a particular action card
  • Turn progress points into sustainability points
